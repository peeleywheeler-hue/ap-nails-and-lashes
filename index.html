About Me

Hiya, I’m Alice, a mobile nail technician based in North Devon 💅 I specialise in gel extensions, BIAB / builder gel, and nail art. I’m mobile, based in Chulmleigh but travel in a 35 minute radius of the surrounding area (Torrington, South Molton, Winkleigh, Witheridge, Burrington etc..) I’m currently welcoming new clients and always aim to provide a relaxed, judgement-free experience, especially if you’re new to nails or a bit nervous.

Whether you're looking for a fresh manicure or stunning nail art,I'm here to make you feel confident and pampered. I use only high-quality products and stay up-to-date with the latest beauty trends and techniques.

My mobile service means you can enjoy professional beauty treatments in the comfort of your own home. Let me bring the salon experience to you!
